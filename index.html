About Me

Thanks to my great love for drawing, photography, as well as art in general, I decided to change my career path and turn to what I enjoy the most.

I graduated from the IT Academy, majoring in Print & Graphic Design, with the intention of mastering programs such as Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and then a course in the field of Web Design (UI/UX design).

I gained my first experiences working on real projects within the Academy, with as many as three winning solutions, 2 logo designs and 1 website design: Šaulina, Vulić, Atlas.

I gained further experience in marketing agencies and online stores where I worked on designing logos, banners as well as designing contents for social networks.
